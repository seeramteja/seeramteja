Yarada village

unseen beauty

Yarada village is a very beautiful village situated in the heart of visakhapatnam, inspite of huge traffice and pollutiion in the city, living style in this village is exactly opposite to what you experience in visakhapatnam, this village is surrounded three sides by hills and one side by bay of bengal. the road to this village is layed only for who are going to this village, there is no connection to other villages,
which makes this village separated from rest of the world except for tourist
